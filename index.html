About Rhythm Aura Dance Academy

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Rhythm Aura Dance Academy emerged from a passionate vision to create a space where dancers of all levels could explore their artistry and connect with their inner rhythm. What started as a small studio with just five instructors has blossomed into a thriving community of over 1000 dancers spanning multiple cities.

Our founder, Maya Sharma, a renowned contemporary dancer with 20+ years of experience, established Rhythm Aura with a single mission: to democratize dance education and make it accessible to everyone, regardless of age, background, or skill level. She believed that dance is not just movement—it's a language of the soul.

Today, Rhythm Aura stands as a beacon of creativity, excellence, and inclusivity. We've trained dancers who have performed on international stages, competed at national championships, and most importantly, discovered their passion for dance. Our academy has become known not just for technical excellence but for fostering a warm, supportive community where every dancer feels valued.

With state-of-the-art studios, world-class instructors, and a curriculum that blends traditional and contemporary styles, we continue to push the boundaries of what dance education can be. Our philosophy is simple: Everyone has rhythm in their heart; we help them find it.

Dance Styles We Teach

Hip Hop

High-energy urban dance with attitude and attitude. Learn popping, locking, breaking, and street styles from professional choreographers.

Contemporary

Express emotion and creativity through fluid, interpretive movements. Blend technique with storytelling and personal expression.

Ballet

The foundation of dance. Master grace, poise, and technique through classical ballet training with certified professionals.

Salsa

Feel the Latin rhythm and passion. Learn authentic salsa moves, partner work, and dance your way to the beat.

Jazz

Energetic, smooth, and versatile. Master groovy movements, isolations, and improvisation in classic jazz style.

K-Pop

Energetic Korean pop choreography. Learn trending K-pop moves and perform to your favorite international hits.

Freestyle

No rules, pure creativity. Express yourself through mixed styles and improvisation without boundaries.

Bharatanatyam

Ancient Indian classical dance form. Learn mudras, hastas, and traditional movements steeped in culture and grace.

Bachata

Romantic Latin dance. Master sensual movements, partner chemistry, and the art of storytelling through dance.
